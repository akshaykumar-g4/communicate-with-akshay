About Me, Your Mentor!

Hello, I'm Akshay. I am the Initiator and Mentor at Communicate with Akshay dedicated to helping people overcome real-world communication challenges. My focus is especially on students in schools and colleges/universities, as well as working professionals in their workplaces and organizations.

With over 3 years of online teaching experience, I have developed an interactive and practical teaching style that goes beyond theory. My sessions emphasize spoken English, impromptu speaking, group discussions, and more—ensuring that you don’t just learn, but actively speak and perform.

My mission is simple: to help you achieve complete fluency and unshakable confidence, so you are fully prepared for any real-life, professional, or social conversation.
